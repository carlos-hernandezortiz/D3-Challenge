Correlations Discovered Between Health Risks and Age, Income

Poverty vs Health Risk

It can be observed that the poorest state have the highest percentage of obesity, however around the mean of poverty, we can see an odd distribution of the obesity vs poverty. The same happens for smokes percentage, when the povery % is higher, the people tend to smoke more. Finally, the most linear comparison is lacks healthcare vs poverty. In the latter, we can see that the poorer the state, the less it is probable to have healthcare.

Age vs Health Risk

It is clear that the most obese states have a median between 36 and 39. This can also be seen for the smokes % and the Lacks Healthcare %.
